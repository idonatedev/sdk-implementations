  • Does KUOW support company matching?

    Yes! Use the company match search tool on the donation page above to see if your employer is already set up to accept matching gifts through our automated system, or you can download a packet (PDF) to take to your HR representative to see if your gift can be matched.

  • Can I edit my monthly Evergreen donation after checkout?
